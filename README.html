It is designed to work alongside my videos on Natural Language Processing and Named Entity Recognition (https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL2VXyKi-KpYs1bSnT8bfMFyGS-wMcjesM). I will be updating this repo regularly during January 2021. In all, there will be somewhere between 20 and 30 notebooks when complete.
